# Drooid — ASO Audit: Executive Summary

**Date:** 2026-06-12 · **Scope:** Apple + Google · **Focus:** Keyword discovery
**Data:** Verified live via iTunes API for Drooid + 3 competitors

---

## The one-sentence takeaway

> Drooid has a genuine, unclaimed differentiator — **AI news summaries** — that its current store listing buries; surfacing it in the title and keyword field is the highest-leverage, lowest-cost ASO move available.

---

## What we found

**1. Apple keyword real estate is being wasted.**
Apple does not index your description. Your searchable terms live only in title + subtitle + a 100-char keyword field. The current title ("News from all sides") spends its weight on a low-search tagline, and the keyword field is likely under-optimized. → Fixed in `02-metadata/apple-metadata.md`.

**2. "AI" and "summaries" are open lanes.**
None of Ground News, AllSides, or SmartNews lead with AI or summarization. Drooid genuinely does both. This is a flanking opportunity against much larger incumbents (Ground News has 44K ratings to Drooid's 54). → Detailed in `01-research/competitor-gaps.md`.

**3. The category's defining term — "balanced news" — is contestable.**
AllSides owns the phrase but has only 295 ratings. Worth contesting directly in the title.

**4. Ratings volume is the real ceiling.**
4.39★ is a healthy *quality* signal, but 54 ratings caps both ranking and conversion. Metadata gets Drooid found; review volume gets it ranked and downloaded.

## Caveats (read before acting)

1. Search volumes are **estimates** — no public API provides them. Relative priorities are reliable; validate exact numbers with your post-change impression data or a paid tool.
2. Confirm your **current subtitle (Apple)** and **full Play listing** in the consoles before overwriting — the API didn't expose those fields.
